How does it work?

SerenityVR is an interactive and immersive visual reality and gameplay environment.

Through the ever expanding array of technologies that allow us to traverse space, some problems are still evident and have huge impacts on astronauts both physically and psychologically. Because of the lack of gravity, muscle loss and bone density loss increase, both causing additional complications; the prolonged seclusion in space away from family and society can cause anxiety and depression. SerenityVR is here to lighten the load. Although astronauts are in a confined space, they donâ€™t have to feel it. Using Unity, Oculus and the Myo armband, SerenityVR brings the user into immersive and interactive environments where they can both exercise through gameplay as well as feel serenity through calming visualizations. SerenityVR not only works for space, but in any industry or place that require a person to be in seclusion or in confined spaces.

Unity3D

Through this powerful physics game engine, stunning visualizations and scenery can be created. Unity provides a platform for creating designs and the logic for interactive games. This is where the magic happens for SerenityVR!

Oculus Rift

The Oculus Rift is a headgear that allowes an individual to be immersed in a visual reality experience. This provides the user vision control in any direction in unity.

Myo Armband

The Myo Armband allows for Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) by tracking the electrical impulses of muscles. The integration of gestures and body motion into Unity provieds the actions necessary to interact with the SerenityVR game environment.

Smartband 2

Although not currently integrated, research has been done for future integration of the smartband 2. This technology will track health and stress levels as well as make suggestions on what SerenityVR environments are most suitable to enter.
